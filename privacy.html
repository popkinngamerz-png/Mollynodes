Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026 · MollyNodes Minecraft Hosting

1. Information We Collect

When you register or use MollyNodes, we collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, username
  • Payment information processed securely through our billing portal
  • Server usage data: resource consumption, uptime logs
  • IP addresses for DDoS protection and security purposes
  • Discord user data when you join our support server (governed by Discord's own policy)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provision, maintain, and improve your hosting services
  • To send billing invoices and service announcements
  • For DDoS mitigation and abuse prevention
  • To provide customer support via Discord
  • To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulations

3. Data Sharing

MollyNodes does not sell or share your personal data with third parties except: (a) as required by Indian law enforcement agencies upon valid legal request; (b) payment processors for billing purposes; (c) with your explicit consent.

4. Data Retention

We retain account data for the duration of your active subscription plus 90 days after termination. Server files and backups are deleted within 72 hours of account closure. Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by law.

5. Cookies

Our web dashboard uses essential cookies for authentication and session management only. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure password hashing, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your personal data, request corrections, request deletion of your account, and withdraw consent at any time. Contact us via Discord or billing portal to exercise these rights.
